OVERVIEW

LAMBORGHINI was developed to amplify your daily routine by anticipating the needs of the driver, making your day-to-day experience behind the wheel unique and totally personal. A state-of-the-art control system and innovative touchscreen easily manage the car’s functions and entertainment system. This, combined with dazzling aerodynamic design, elevate the performance and driving emotions to unprecedented levels

URUS: THE SUPER SPORTS CAR FOR ANY SURFACE

Urus is the first Super SUV in the world to combine the performance of a super sports car with the versatility of an SUV. A vehicle of extreme proportions in which the unmistakable Lamborghini DNA is expressed with a design inspired by the Aventador and the LM002. With Urus, Automobili Lamborghini carries forward its tradition as an innovating brand that breaks out of the mold, creating extraordinary and pioneering vehicles that make the impossible possible.
