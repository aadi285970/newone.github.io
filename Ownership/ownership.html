THE ULTIMATE QUALITY FOR YOUR LAMBORGHINI

To be considered eligible for the program, a pre-owned Lamborghini must meet the following criteria:

  • Have no more than 70,000 km/43,500 miles of use (Urus 100,000 kms/62,000 miles)
  • Have less than 84 months of use (Urus â€“ 120 months)
  • Have undergone the regular scheduled maintenance indicated by Automobili Lamborghini
  • Have passed a complete checklist test

To be part of the Selezione Lamborghini Certified Pre-Owned Program, each vehicle must pass over 150 checks.

The inspection covers all the car's main features. For instance:

  • Vehicle history
  • Vehicle interior & exterior, including luggage compartment
  • Fluid levels
  • Mechanics and electronics
  • Tires, rims and brakes

THE SELEZIONE LAMBORGHINI CERTIFIED PRE-OWNED WARRANTY

Every Selezione Certified Pre-Owned vehicle is covered by a minimum of 12 months and up to 24 months of additional Lamborghini warranty protection. The protection covers parts and labour, and ensures all the operations are performed by trained Lamborghini technicians and with original parts only.


ALWAYS SAFE ON YOUR WAY: THE ROADSIDE ASSISTANCE


To ensure total peace of mind, each Selezione Certified Pre-Owned vehicle is offered with roadside assistance for the entire duration of the warranty. The service is available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year*.
